# 8.5" × 11" paper

Use instead of *8.5-by-11-inch, 8.5 × 11-inch, 8 1/2 by 11-inch,*
or other ways of referring to the paper size. Use a
straight quotation mark (") to signify inches. Use a
multiplication sign (×), not the letter *x.*
